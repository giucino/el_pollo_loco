Class: World

World(canvas, keyboard)

A world is a collection of objects that are drawn on a canvas. It is responsible for drawing all objects and for handling the game logic. The world is also responsible for the camera movement. A world has a level, a character, an endboss, a chick, a status bar for the health of the character,
